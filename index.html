"I am a sophomore at Pennsylvania State University studying Human Centered Design and Development. My career goal is to help foster a professional environment
where field data, UI design, and software engineering can all seamlessly blend together to create positive user experiences.
I have recently started the application focus portion of my degree which allows us to explore an alternative field of study that we believe we can apply to our
career. I chose Healthcare because I am deeply passionate about taking out the bureaucracy and confusion that patients of the US
healthcare system may find online. When it comes to both mental and physical health, I want to find ways to make the user experience of those trying to care of themselves a positive one.
Last semester I worked on a group project where I designed a mobile application that centralized multiple online mental health resources into a single application.
This project took us through the design thinking stages of empathizing, defining, ideating, prototyping, test, and implementing
which ultimately resulted in a Figma prototype. I am currently programming this concept to life and look forward to working on more projects where I can explore user experience and UI
design within the world of Healthcare and other fields."
